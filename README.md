# Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes sales data and visualizes key business metrics using Power BI.

## Objectives
- Analyze sales trends
- Identify top-performing products
- Monitor revenue KPIs
- Evaluate regional performance

## Tools Used
- Power BI
- Excel
- Python (Data Cleaning and PreProcessing)

## **Key Insights**
Sales trend: Revenue grew from 8M (2024) to a peak of 14M (2025), then dropped to 6M in 2026 — likely reflects a partial 2026 year rather than an actual decline.
Customer growth: Customer count rose steadily from 55 (2022) to a peak of 99 (2024), held flat at 98 (2025), then fell to 42 (2026), consistent with an incomplete current year.
Order fulfillment risk: Returned (21.97%) and Cancelled (19.91%) orders together account for over 41% of all orders — a significant share that points to potential issues in product quality, checkout, or delivery experience.
Sales by gender: Male customers lead in sales (11M), followed by Female (9M) and Other (7M), showing a relatively balanced demographic spread.
Geographic reach: Customers span North America, Europe, and Asia, indicating a multi-region customer base.
Product stock: Top products by stock quantity are tightly clustered (495–499 units), suggesting even inventory distribution across the catalog.
